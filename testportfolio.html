The End of my Portfolio.

AP Computer Science Principles fundamentally changed my perception of computer programming. I now know how to program in Python, how to build websites in HTML/CSS, how to create simulations with Netlogo, and how to make android apps with MIT App Inventor. Over the course of the semester, I worked with many people who I would like to thank for their hard work and effort. I am thankful for this because the most important thing I learned in this class was how to push myself into putting in extra hours into my projects just for knowledge and a great product, instead of a good grade. Computer Science Principles was a great class and I can't wait to do AP Computer Science A in the future!
